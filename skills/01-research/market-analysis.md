# Skill: Market Analysis

## Goal
Translate the idea into market context and opportunity clarity.

## When To Use
After project classification and before defining the MVP.

## Inputs
- `project-input/idea.md`
- `project-input/target-users.md`
- `project-input/references.md`

## Steps
1. Define the target segment.
2. Identify the painful workflow or unmet need.
3. Identify alternatives users already use.
4. Estimate willingness to pay.
5. Identify the fastest value wedge.
6. Note one strong positioning angle.

## Output
- target segment
- main pain point
- current alternatives
- urgency level
- willingness to pay estimate
- best positioning angle
- fast-entry opportunity

## Constraints
- Do not create fake precision.
- Do not turn the analysis into a long report.
- Stay focused on practical product decisions.

## Success Criteria
The result reveals why the product deserves to exist and how it can enter the market quickly.
