# Skill: Project Classifier

## Goal
Determine the exact category and delivery shape of the project.

## When To Use
Use at the beginning of every new project.

## Inputs
- `project-input/idea.md`
- `project-input/target-users.md`
- `project-input/constraints.md`
- `project-input/monetization.md`

## Steps
1. Identify the core problem.
2. Identify the primary user.
3. Determine the product type:
   - SaaS
   - AI SaaS
   - Marketplace
   - Internal tool
   - Consumer app
   - RAG assistant
   - Workflow automation
4. Determine the revenue model.
5. Determine operational complexity.
6. Assign the best playbook.

## Output
- project category
- product type
- target user
- revenue shape
- complexity level
- recommended playbook
- project risks

## Constraints
- Do not classify based on hype words alone.
- Do not assume AI is required unless it adds measurable value.

## Success Criteria
A builder can read the classification and immediately understand what kind of product is being built.
