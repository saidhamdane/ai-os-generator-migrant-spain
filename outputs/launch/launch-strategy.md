# Launch Strategy — MigrantCopilot Spain

## Core Positioning

**For:** Arabic-speaking immigrants in Spain in their first year
**Who need:** Clear guidance on what to do next in Spain's bureaucracy
**The product is:** An AI-assisted action planner that gives them a personalized, step-by-step plan in Arabic and Spanish
**Unlike:** Government websites, generic legal blogs, or WhatsApp advice from friends
**Ours:** Personalized to their exact situation, in their language, with the next step always obvious

---

## Ideal Early Adopter Profile

- Arabic-speaking immigrant (Moroccan, Algerian, Egyptian, Syrian — by population size in Spain)
- Arrived in Spain in the last 6 months
- Has not yet completed empadronamiento, NIE, or TIE
- Active on Facebook groups, WhatsApp, and Telegram communities for Arab immigrants in Spain
- Willing to pay €10–15/month if the product solves a real, immediate problem
- Likely referral: tells family members or community contacts who are also about to arrive

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–2): Audience Seeding

**Goal:** Build a waitlist of 200+ people before launch.

**Action: Identify and join target communities**

Facebook Groups (join and observe before posting):
- "المغاربة في إسبانيا" (Moroccans in Spain) — typically 50k–200k members
- "الجزائريون في إسبانيا" (Algerians in Spain)
- "عرب إسبانيا" (Arabs in Spain — general)
- "المهاجرون في مدريد" (Immigrants in Madrid)
- "المهاجرون في برشلونة" (Immigrants in Barcelona)
- Groups specific to cities: Valencia, Seville, Málaga

Telegram channels:
- Search "مهاجرين إسبانيا", "عرب مدريد", "مغاربة برشلونة"
- Join 5–10 active channels

**Action: Post educational content — not ads**

Post format (Arabic):
```
سؤال شائع يسألني كثير من المهاجرين الجدد:
ما أول شيء يجب أن أفعله بعد وصولي لإسبانيا؟

الإجابة البسيطة:
1. التسجيل في البلدية (Empadronamiento)
2. طلب رقم NIE
3. التسجيل في الضمان الاجتماعي

لكن كل شخص يختلف حسب وضعه...

هل تريد خطة شخصية مجانية بناءً على وضعك؟
اترك تعليقاً أو راسلني.
```

**Goal of pre-launch posts:** Collect comments, DMs, emails. Redirect to a simple waitlist page (Tally.so or direct email collection on landing page).

## Phase 3 — Content Engine (Weeks 3–8)

**Goal:** Organic growth through educational Arabic-language content.

**Platform:** TikTok + Instagram Reels (Arabic)

**Content format:** Short video (30–60 seconds), talking head or screen share, Arabic voiceover

**5 video ideas to produce before launch:**

1. "أول 5 خطوات لأي مهاجر جديد في إسبانيا"
   (First 5 steps for any new immigrant in Spain)

2. "الخطأ الأكثر شيوعاً عند طلب NIE — وكيف تتجنبه"
   (Most common NIE mistake and how to avoid it)

3. "كيف تفتح حساباً بنكياً في إسبانيا بدون وثائق إقامة"
   (How to open a bank account in Spain without residency papers)

4. "ما هو Empadronamiento ولماذا هو أهم شيء تفعله أولاً"
   (What is Empadronamiento and why it's the first thing you must do)

5. "وثائق يجب أن تجهزها قبل حجز موعد NIE"
   (Documents you must prepare before booking your NIE appointment)

**Each video CTA:** "رابط في البايو — احصل على خطتك الشخصية مجاناً"
(Link in bio — get your personal plan for free)

**Posting schedule:** 3 videos/week in first month, 2/week after.

## Landing Page Structure

**Hero:**
- Headline (AR): "اعرف ماذا تفعل الآن في إسبانيا"
- Headline (ES): "Sabe exactamente qué hacer ahora en España"
- Subheadline: "أداة شخصية تحول الإجراءات المعقدة إلى خطوات واضحة — بالعربية والإسبانية"
- CTA: "ابدأ مجاناً" / "Empieza gratis"

**Pain section (3 bullets):**
- هل تشعر بالضياع في الإجراءات البيروقراطية؟
- هل لا تعرف ماذا تفعل أولاً؟
- هل تخاف من تفويت موعد أو نسيان وثيقة مهمة؟

**How it works (3 steps):**
1. أخبرنا بوضعك (Tell us your situation — 5 minutes)
2. نولّد خطتك الشخصية (We generate your personalized plan)
3. تتبع خطواتك خطوة بخطوة (Track your steps one by one)

**Social proof placeholder:** "انضم إلى [X] مهاجر يستخدمون الأداة الآن"
(Replace X with real number at launch — even 12 users is honest social proof)

**FAQ (3 questions):**
- هل الأداة مجانية؟ (Is it free?)
- هل المعلومات دقيقة؟ (Is the information accurate?)
- ما اللغات المتاحة؟ (What languages are available?)

## Pricing Strategy

**At launch:**
- Free plan: limited roadmap (2 steps), limited checklist (3 items)
- Pro plan: **€9.99/month** (test price)
- No annual plan until month 2 (validate monthly first)

**Upgrade trigger points** (show upgrade CTA at these moments):
1. After viewing step 2 in roadmap: "See step 3 and beyond"
2. After viewing 3rd checklist item: "See all your required documents"
3. On Reminders page: "Upgrade to get email reminders for your deadlines"
4. On landing page for direct traffic: always visible

**Price increase plan:**
- Month 1: €9.99/month (early adopter price)
- Month 3: test €12.99/month with annual option (€89/year = save 43%)
- Do not raise price until 20+ paying users confirm value

## First Revenue Target

**Week 3 (launch week):** 5 paying users = €50/month MRR
**Month 1 end:** 25 paying users = €250/month MRR
**Month 3 end:** 100 paying users = €999/month MRR

These are validation targets, not growth projections.

**Signal to continue:** If 10 users pay without being asked personally → product-market fit signal.
**Signal to pivot copy:** If signups happen but zero upgrades → value proposition not landing; interview free users.

## Post-Launch Feedback Loop

**After intake, ask (displayed on roadmap page, first visit):**

Questions shown as 1-click ratings or text boxes:
1. "هل كانت خطتك واضحة؟" — Was your plan clear? [Yes / Somewhat / No]
2. "ما الخطوة التي تشعر بالتوتر منها أكثر؟" — Which step makes you most anxious? [text]
3. "ما الوثيقة التي لا تعرف كيف تحصل عليها؟" — Which document are you unsure how to get? [text]

**Where to store:** Simple form → Resend email to founder inbox. No complex analytics in v1.

**Act on answers:**
- Common anxious step → improve that procedure guide
- Common missing document question → add clearer notes to checklist item
- "Plan wasn't clear" → revisit roadmap generation logic or onboarding copy

## Legal Disclaimer Strategy

**Where:** Footer of every page and top of every procedure guide.

**Text (short version, Arabic):**
"هذه الأداة تقدم معلومات إرشادية عامة فقط وليست استشارة قانونية. تحقق دائماً من المعلومات الرسمية على الموقع الحكومي المعني."

**Text (short version, Spanish):**
"Esta herramienta proporciona información orientativa general y no constituye asesoramiento legal. Verifica siempre la información en el sitio oficial correspondiente."

**Positioning of disclaimer:** Trust-building, not fear-creating. Frame as: "We help you understand — official offices confirm." Do not let disclaimer language undermine conversion.
